    Welcome to Tennis Knowledge Base¶

    This is a bilingual tennis resource — English and Vietnamese — built from the ground up for players who want more than just tips and drills. Here you'll find the why behind the techniques, the neural control theory that explains how movement actually works, and the principles that transfer across strokes.

    How to Use This Site¶

    Everything is reachable in 3 clicks or less:

    1. Click your language → English or Vietnamese
    2. Choose your level → Foundation (basics), Advanced (pattern/tactics), or Elite (advanced concepts)
    3. Pick a topic → Drills, deep dives, or specific techniques

    No dead ends. No mysterious folders. Just clear paths to what you need.

    About This Site¶

    This knowledge base grew out of frustration with surface-level tennis instruction. Most coaching tells you what to do — "get under the ball," "hit through it," "use your legs" — but never explains why or how your nervous system actually learns these patterns.

    Everything here is grounded in: - Raw coaching materials — no AI-generated fluff - Bilingual by design — not translated, but written natively in both languages - Neural control theory — understanding movement as the brain learns it - Practical application — drills and progressions you can use today

    For educational purposes only. Not a substitute for professional coaching.
