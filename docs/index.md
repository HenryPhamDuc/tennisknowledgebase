## About This Site

This knowledge base grew out of frustration with surface-level tennis instruction. Most coaching tells you *what* to do — "get under the ball," "hit through it," "use your legs" — but never explains *why* or *how* your nervous system actually learns these patterns.

Everything here is grounded in:
- **Raw coaching materials** — no AI-generated fluff
- **Bilingual by design** — not translated, but written natively in both languages
- **Neural control theory** — understanding movement as the brain learns it
- **Practical application** — drills and progressions you can use today

For educational purposes only. Not a substitute for professional coaching.
