# Advanced

The advanced tier is for players who have solid fundamentals and want to understand **tactics**, develop **pattern recognition**, and build a **mental framework** for competitive play. This is where technique meets strategy.

## What's Here

- **Tactical patterns** — High-percentage tennis, shot selection, and constructing points
- **Mental game** — Pressure management, decision-making under stress, avoiding choking
- **Doubles strategy** — Positioning, communication, and tactical systems for teams
- **Neural control** — How proprioception and reflexes actually work, not just "feel"
- **Self-coaching** — How to diagnose and fix your own problems between coaching sessions
