# Elite

The elite tier is for players and coaches who want to understand the **philosophy** and **science** behind skill acquisition. This is where we move beyond "how to hit" and into **how humans learn movement**.

## What's Here

- **Anti-orthodox manifesto** — Questioning conventional coaching wisdom
- **Constraint-led approach** — Designing practice that guides discovery instead of prescribing answers
- **Embodied cognition** — Understanding that your body doesn't just execute commands — it thinks
- **Self-coaching systems** — Building frameworks for continuous improvement without constant external input
- **Neural control theory** — The real science behind "feel" and proprioception
- **The dream library** — A personal knowledge system for integrating tennis and life
