# Foundation

The foundation tier covers the fundamentals that every tennis player needs. Whether you're just starting or looking to rebuild your game from the ground up, these pages explain the **why** behind each technique, not just the **what**.

## What's Here

- **Grip fundamentals** — Continental, Eastern, Western: when and why each matters
- **Forehand mechanics** — The kinetic chain, pronation, and finding consistency
- **Backhand patterns** — One-hand vs. two-hand, slice as both defensive and offensive weapon
- **Footwork essentials** — Split-step, recovery, and positioning without wasted movement
- **Serve basics** — Platform vs. pinpoint, trophy position, and finding your natural motion
